You have 3 options to play a Multiplayer game:

  • Join a public game
    • You will join with a random player
  • Create a private game
    • You can create a game and let your QR-code be scanned by a friend, or you can share your code trough social media (only available on Android)
  • Join a private game
    • Join a game of your friend by scanning his QR-code (QR-code scanner only available on Android) or just enter the code.
